Why Agrotech Solutions ?

A one stop service provider for your spraying, crop health monitoring, soil testing and nutrient input identification. Services

Automating time consuming, laborious and unorganized farming activities such as chemical discharge, crop scouting and soil testing using unmanned aerial vehicles, multispectral cameras and electrochemical sensors. We offer two distinct services - Integrated Chemical Management and Crop/Soil Health Management.

​Organic or Inorganic chemical spray

Deploy unmanned aerial vehicle capable of discharging liquid. Focussing on uniform or variable rate application of required chemical + water solutions.

Spray activity monitoring

Unmanned aerial vehicle software enables us to track amount of liquid sprayed (chemical and water respectively or as a whole) and operational time.

On-site visit + consultation

Farm visit arranged upon primary contact for service to perform survey and generate work assessment report. Agronomist and agriculture experts consultation available regularly.

Crop/Soil health reporting

Deploy unmanned aerial vehicle equipped with multispectral cameras to collect-analyze orthomosaic maps and report findings. Soil testing using sensors at intervals as required

​Chemical supply

Provide inventory of required chemicals directly from manufacturers.
